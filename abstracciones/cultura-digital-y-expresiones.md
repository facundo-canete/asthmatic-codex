# Cultura Digital y Expresiones

## Ball-knowledge

### Definition:
Internet slang that refers to a deep, sophisticated or expert understanding of a specific subject. While it originally meant having a high 'game IQ' in sports like basketball or football (soccer), the slang since evolved into a flexible meme.


## Rabbit hole

### Definition:
1. A situation in which you become so interested in a subject or an activity that you cannot stop trying to find out about it or doing it;
2. A complicated or difficult situation, especially one that is difficult to get out of.
