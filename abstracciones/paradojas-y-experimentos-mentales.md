## Gato de Schrödinger

### Definición:
Experimento mental, a veces descrito como una paradoja. El escenario presenta un gato hipotético que puede estar simultáneamente vivo y muerto, un estado conocido como *superposición cuántica*, como resultado de estar vinculado a un evento subatómico aleatorio que puede ocurrir o no.


## Grellin-Nelson paradox

### Definition:
This paradox arises when we consider the adjective 'heterological'. One can ask: is 'heterological' a heterological word? If the answer is 'no', then 'heterological' is autological. This leads to a contradiction, for in this case 'heterological' does not describe itself: it must be a heterological word. But if the answer is 'yes', the word 'heterological' is heterological. This again leads to a contradiction, because if the word 'heterological' describes itself, it is autological.

## Ship of Theseus or Theseus's paradox

### Definition:
It's a paradox and common thought experiment about whether an object (in the most common stating of the paradox, a ship) is the same object after having all of its original components replaced with others over time.
