# Phrases

## 'Hit the road'

### Definition:
To leave a place or begin a journey; e.g., 'I'd love to stay longer but I must be hitting the road'.


## 'Thinking about the immortality of the crab'

### Definition:
Spanish idiom referring to the act of daydreaming. It is a humorous way to say that one was not sitting idly but engaged constructively in contemplation or letting one's mind wander.
