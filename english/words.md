## Accession

### Definitions:

1. The time when someone starts a position of authority, especially a king or queen; e.g., '1926 was the year of Emperor Hirohito's accession to the throne';
2. The ntime when a country officially joins a group of countries or signs an agreement; e.g., 'Poland's accession to the EU';
3. An addition to a collection, such as a book or a work of art; e.g., 'There are several new accessions to the library'.

## Autological

### Definition:
An autological word expresses a property that it also possesses. A word which is not autological is heterological, except for the word 'heterological' itself; q.v. Grelling-Nelson paradox.

## Bigot

### Definitions:

1. A person who has strong, unreasonable beliefs and who does not like other people who have different beliefs or a different way of life; e.g., a religious bigot, 'He was known to be a loud-mouthed, opinionated bigot';
2. Somebody who dislikes others because of their race, religion or politics.
